ACTION REQUIRED: Immediate Identity Verification

Due to a recent increase in fraudulent claims, Medicare requires all beneficiaries to verify their identity to continue receiving benefits. Failure to verify within 48 hours will result in immediate suspension of your Medicare coverage. This verification is mandatory per CMS regulation 42 CFR § 422.504.
