IMMEDIATE ACTION REQUIRED

Verify your identity within 48 hours to avoid suspension of your tax refund and potential penalties. Due to recent security updates, the IRS requires identity verification to continue processing your tax refund. Failure to verify within 48 hours will result in refund delay and possible audit.

Why Verification Is Required

To protect taxpayers and combat identity theft, the IRS has implemented enhanced security protocols for all refund processing. This one-time verification confirms your identity and ensures your refund is issued securely.

  • Prevents fraudulent refund claims filed under your identity
  • Ensures compliance with IRS Publication 1075 security standards
  • Required before your refund can be approved and disbursed
